ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Management Studies at JP Institute of Technology, established in 1995, addresses the growing demand for top-tier management professionals by offering high-quality education to shape ethical, analytical, and visionary leaders. The department provides an autonomous MBA program with an annual intake of 300 students, including 240 for the 2-year MBA and 60 for the 5 years Integrated MBA. The MBA department received ₹51 lakhs from various funding agencies like ICSSR, Government of Tamil Nadu, National Commission for Women and Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development for the last 3 years. Over ₹3 lakhs fund generated through consultancy projects of various companies like Apollo Tyres, Cyril Sea Foods, Dinesh Exports, Team Pro and so on. 450+ research articles are published in reputed journals, the department excels in integrating academics with industry partnerships like Zoho, IBM, WNS, CII, BYST, Imarticus and so on. Students have secured 100% placements in national and international companies like ICICI Bank, Innocap, Federal Bank, Naukri, BNY Mellon, Bruhat Logistics and so on with the highest package of 15 lakhs. Students are provided with practical exposure through internships, corporate trainings, projects and guest lectures to meet the industry requirements.


VISION OF THE DEPARTMENT

To establish a dynamic platform for developing the aspiring managers to meet the industry challenges, promote entrepreneurship and research to enhance the Institution reputation.

MISSION OF THE DEPARTMENT

  • M1: To provide a transformative education that blends practical experiences and theoretical knowledge, to face the business challenges.
  • M2: To cultivate a collaborative research environment that promotes social responsibility and upholds ethical practices in business.
  • M3: To build a partnership with industries, ensuring the programs remain relevant and impactful in the global economy.
  • M4: To inspire, educate, and empower students to become innovative thinkers and resilient entrepreneurs by providing a supportive environment
